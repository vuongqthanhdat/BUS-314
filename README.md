# BUS-314
## Organisational Behaviour
This course provides students with the knowledge, skills, and abilities to manage and lead others now and in the future effectively. The course moves progressively through individual, group, and organisational levels of behaviour, drawing on concepts and practices from the field of organisational behaviour (OB). It examines the interrelationship of behavioural phenomena among these levels and across different business functions, providing opportunities for students to integrate interests from various business majors.
